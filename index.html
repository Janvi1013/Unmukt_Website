About Unmukt

Who We Are

Unmukt is more than a brand—it is a movement. Every product represents the dreams, skills, resilience, and determination of rural women who are redefining their futures.

We work at the grass-roots level in Rajasthan, bridging the gap between traditional handicraft techniques and contemporary global markets. Our collective equips women with specialized skills, raw materials, design guidelines, and business literacy to turn their artistry into financial freedom.

“We turn textile waste into beautiful handmade products, empowering women and protecting the planet.”

Our Mission

To foster sustainable economic self-reliance among rural women artisans by providing high-quality skill training, fair-wage employment, and premium market linkages.

Our Vision

An inclusive society where rural women lead with pride, their traditional crafts are preserved, and every artisan family lives with financial security and dignity.

The Journey

How Unmukt Was Born

A transition from urban tech to rural crafts that evolved into a thriving women-led movement.

Jitendra Singh

Founder & Director
"I realized that true innovation isn't just about code and servers; it is about human dignity, community enrichment, and unleashing the immense talent that already exists in rural India."

Jitendra left a rewarding engineering career to follow a calling: creating sustainable livelihoods in the heartland of Rajasthan. Identifying a lack of stable work for women, he established skill workshops that quickly blossomed. Today, he steers Unmukt's strategic vision, building B2B corporate partnerships that sustain hundreds of rural families.

2021

Stepping out of the Comfort Zone

Jitendra Singh makes the pivotal choice to leave his corporate engineering job, relocating to rural Rajasthan to research grassroots structural problems and agricultural dry-spells.

2022 (Q1)

The "She Dares" Initiative

Launches She Dares—a pilot group targeting basic financial literacy and community leadership dialogues among village women.

2022 (Q3)

Uncovering Latent Craft Talents

Discovering that many women possess ancestral knowledge of intricate hand embroidery, sewing, and stitching, hidden due to lack of market access.

2023

Structured Skill Development

Forming professional training clusters. Master artisans are brought in to refine stitching, quality standards, and teach contemporary product shapes.

2024

Livelihood Scale-up

Setting up steady payment infrastructures and introducing upcycled fabric lines, turning leftover materials into beautiful design toys and eco-friendly products.

2025 - Present

The Birth of Unmukt

The collective takes the official brand name "Unmukt" (meaning 'liberated'). It stands as a premium social enterprise supplying B2B buyers and enabling hundreds of artisans to thrive independently.

🌿 Proudly Environment Friendly

We transform textile waste into beautiful handmade products — empowering rural women while protecting our planet. Every stitch is a step towards a greener, more sustainable future.
