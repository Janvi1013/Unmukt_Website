Empowerment Through Skills

Training & Capacity Building

Our hubs provide rural women with craft training, quality systems, financial planning, and leadership workshops.

Stitching & Tailoring

Machine sewing, fabric layout sizing, edge trims, and advanced pattern assembly for high durability.

Suf Embroidery

Refining traditional needle patterns using freehand geometry and fine threads on organic canvas panels.

Quality Control

Strict auditing for measurement ranges, stitch density, clean trims, and export pack certifications.

Product Development

Translating traditional motifs into functional products like upcycled toys, bags, and cushion covers.

Financial Literacy

Setting up self-managed bank accounts, reading passbooks, digital savings, and microfinance planning.

Digital Literacy

Managing smartphone apps, accepting UPI mobile transfers, digital inventory logs, and communications.

Micro-Enterprise

Forming local artisan hubs, tracking cluster timesheets, packaging logistics, and running cluster audits.

Leadership & Org

Public speaking, solving community conflicts, and representing village clusters at municipal blocks.

Our Footprint

Expanding Training Access

We work in collaboration with the People's Awareness Network Society (PANS) and Lamberti India, setting up community hubs that reduce travel times for rural women, allowing them to balance family care with paid skill workshops.

Over 100+ training modules have been executed across 7 villages, developing 250+ unique handcrafted product designs.
